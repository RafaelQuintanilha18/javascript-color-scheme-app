The ColorPicker App

What is it?

An awesome tool to help you find the best color scheme for your app. Integrated with The Color API, you have more than 2000 colors and 8 different color schemes to choose from.

How does it work

1) Click at the square to open a color selector and choose the main color for your color scheme
2) Select the color scheme you want from the dropdown menu
3) Click the "GET COLOR SCHEME" button and the app will generate the color pallet based on the color and scheme selected ðŸ˜‰
